## Notes on Engagement Strategy

**City of Hamilton:** Focus on infrastructure planning, asset management, and emergency preparedness perspectives. Key concern: $5.36B infrastructure investment and stormwater utility launch (April 2026).

**Conservation Authority:** Essential for floodplain mapping, watershed data, and regulatory framework. They operate flood control infrastructure and conduct flood forecasting.

**Advocacy Groups:** Can provide community perspective, environmental justice concerns, and help identify vulnerable populations. Strong focus on climate adaptation and resilience.

**Insurance Industry:** Offer financial risk perspective, claims data, underwriting challenges, and insurance product design insights. Currently grappling with increasing flood losses and national flood insurance program development.

**Academic/Research Community:** Provide modelling expertise, data analysis capabilities, and connections to cutting-edge research. McMaster's proximity to Hamilton makes them ideal partners.
